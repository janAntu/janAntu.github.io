Who Am I?

By Richard Andrus on 11 October 2020

About Me

I am a fourth-year student at UC San Diego finishing my B.S. Computer Science, with a focus on Big Data systems. This field includes everything from databases, cloud systems, networking, parallelism, machine learning and more, and I've gained experience with these subjects both here at UCSD and from internships. So far, I've worked at the Statistical Visual Computing Lab at UCSD creating data visualization tools; at Knowt, where I developed text analysis algorithms; and at Qualcomm, where I built data pipelines for machine learning. After graduating, I hope to continue working at Qualcomm and developing my skills as a data engineer.

About Me

I'm currently living in Phelan California, a small town in the mountains northeast of Los Angeles.
